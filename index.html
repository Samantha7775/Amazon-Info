Here I am gonna provide you with a little bit of information about Amazon Affiliate marketing


  1. What is Amazon Affiliate Marketing?

    2. It is Affiliate Marketing Program. It is free for Website owners and Bloggers who want to associate with Amazon . They advertise products from Amazon or give products link on their site and when customers click on their given link and buy a product they will get referral fees.


  2. Does Amazon Affiliate pay for click?

    3. No, Amazon Affiliate is not like pay-per click type program. Which means you will only get paid whenever you generate a sale.


  3. Why Amazon sellers use the Affiliate Program?

    4. Amazon Affiliate Marketing Program is beneficial for sellers as its help in conducting sales . Its brings customer at your product. For this program you must be a registered seller after taking this step, you'll pay monthly seller fees in addition to various other costs, like referral fees and fulfillment fees. Referral fees are where the Affiliate Program comes in.


  4. Who is eligible to be an Amazon Affiliate?

    5. Amazon is open to affiliates from all over the world. You don’t need to pay a registration fee to join, nor do you need to maintain a quota to earn your commissions. Once Amazon approves your application to be an affiliate, you can advertise their goods right away


  5. How much an Affiliate can earn?

    6. You can earn, on average, from $100 to $20,000 from the Amazon Affiliate program, depending on how many referrals you generate for Amazon. The Amazon Affiliate program operates on a commission basis, meaning you'll make a percentage per sale. The commission rate differs depending on the product category.
